VAWiki is a web portal containing many posts, online artifacts, news, and laws on the current struggle of women against VAW

Vision of the project

We hope to be able to achieve the following goals with this portal:

  • Improve community awareness on Violence Against Women or VAW
  • Raise issues in the community through online interaction
  • Improve working and living conditions for marginalized women locally and globally
  • Give importance to the known and unheard traumas of women in society
  • Neutralize the stigma against feminism

    • In this, we have Dr. Rhodora Bucoy to thank for all the lessons and firsthand recounts of various experiences that have shaped us as we have taken up her course.
