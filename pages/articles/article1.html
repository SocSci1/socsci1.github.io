What's this page for?

December 5, 2017 by Juan Carlos Roldan

This portal is a social endeavor made by Computer Science students of UP Cebu taking up a Foundations of Behavioral Science class under Dr. Rhodora Bucoy.

It's a project requirement for the class, yes. But also, we wanted to demonstrate how best to show our expertise in the fields of IT to bridge the issues regarding women and class struggles to the community.

As Emma Watson, UN Ambassador for Women once said in a speech: If not me, who? If not now, when? Let us take heed.

People like Emma Watson understand that feminism is not equal to manhating. We endeavor to build understanding of the issue by exposing stories of women, urging people to take a stand on something dearly important.
